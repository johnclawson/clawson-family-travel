- **Highlights:** USS Midway, Balboa Park, La Jolla tide pools, San Diego Zoo, beaches
- **Weather (April/May):** 68-72¬∞F, ideal
- **Notes:** Original plan ‚Äî 1 room on points (~60K Bonvoy), second room cash (~$500)

### Mobile/Gulf Shores, AL
- **Airport:** MOB (connect via ATL)
- **Flight time:** ~5 hours total
- **Best Marriott options:** Fairfield Inn Gulf Shores (Cat 3, ~20K/night), SpringHill Suites Orange Beach (Cat 4, ~25K/night)
- **Highlights:**
  - USS Alabama Battleship Memorial Park (WWII battleship + submarine + aircraft)
  - Gulf Shores/Orange Beach (white sand, calm Gulf waters)
  - Fort Morgan (Civil War history)
  - Gulf State Park (hiking, biking, kayaking)
  - Alabama Gulf Coast Zoo
- **Weather (April/May):** 75-85¬∞F, water warm enough for swimming
- **Notes:** Best combination of battleship history + beaches. Lower hotel categories stretch Bonvoy further.

### Outer Banks, NC
- **Airport:** ORF - Norfolk (85 min drive to Kill Devil Hills)
- **Flight time:** ~5.5 hours total (connect via ATL)
- **Marriott options:** Very limited ‚Äî Airbnb beach houses are the norm and better fit
- **Highlights:**
  - Wright Brothers National Memorial (where flight was born)
  - Corolla Wild Horses (wild Spanish mustangs on beach)
  - Cape Hatteras Lighthouse (climb 257 steps)
  - Jockey's Ridge State Park (tallest sand dunes on East Coast)
  - Roanoke Island / Lost Colony (America's first English settlement)
  - 70 miles of undeveloped beaches at Cape Hatteras National Seashore
- **Weather (April/May):** 65-75¬∞F, pleasant but water still cool
- **Notes:** Perfect for family's Airbnb preference. Unique history not found elsewhere.

### Wilmington / Wrightsville Beach, NC
- **Airport:** ILM - Wilmington (connect via ATL)
- **Flight time:** ~5 hours total
- **Best Marriott options:** Courtyard Wilmington/Wrightsville Beach (Cat 4, ~25-35K/night), Fairfield Inn Wilmington (Cat 3, ~20K/night)
- **Highlights:**
  - USS North Carolina Battleship (WWII, 9 decks to explore ‚Äî similar to USS Alabama)
  - Historic Downtown Wilmington (Riverwalk, antebellum architecture)
  - Wrightsville Beach (clean family beach)
  - Fort Fisher State Historic Site (Civil War)
  - Carolina Beach State Park (Venus flytraps native here!)
- **Weather (April/May):** 70-78¬∞F
- **Notes:** Could combine with Outer Banks for 7-10 day trip (4.5 hour drive between them)

### Washington DC
- **Airport:** DCA - Reagan National (nonstop from SLC on Delta, Metro accessible)
- **Flight time:** ~4 hours
- **Best Marriott options:** Residence Inn Capitol (Cat 5, ~30-40K/night for suite that fits 6)
- **Highlights:**
  - Smithsonian Museums (ALL FREE):
    - National Air and Space Museum (Wright Flyer, Apollo 11, Space Shuttle)
    - Natural History Museum (dinosaurs, Hope Diamond)
    - American History Museum (Star-Spangled Banner)
    - National Zoo (pandas, great cats)
  - National Mall Monuments (Lincoln, WWII, Vietnam, MLK, Jefferson)
  - Arlington National Cemetery (Changing of the Guard, JFK eternal flame)
  - National Archives (Declaration of Independence, Constitution)
  - Capitol Building Tour (free, book through congressperson)
- **Weather (April/May):** 65-75¬∞F
- **Ground transportation:** NO RENTAL CAR NEEDED ‚Äî Metro covers everything
- **Notes:** Higher hotel points but dramatically lower cash outlay. Free attractions save $500-1,000 vs other destinations.
